Spring Soirée

V

OVERVIEW:

Collaborating with the Human Resources Student's Association (HRSA), which is a student organization at Simon Fraser University's Beedie School of Business, I functioned as their main designer for their pillar event, Spring Soirée; I created informational packages within 1 week that would be sent out to HR professionals. HRSA's annual stand-up networking event, Spring Soirée, explores topics surrounding Human Resources.

PROCESS:

Given this design:

I began to re-organize the layout as I found that the colours were too strong, the font is static and does not engage with the reader, and the structure of content does not invite the audience to read through the package. Taking inspiration from their webpage, I utilized their colour palette to ensure that branding would be consistent with the overall club.

CHALLENGES:

Communicating with such a large club was very difficult in terms of creating a finalize package design. I had to wait for weeks for the Corporate and External coordinators, who were responsible for the copywrite to finish as they were not meeting the deliverable deadlines. Therefore, not only did I fabricated all 3 packages on the spot, but I also took the initiative to work with the Vice President of Events to finish writing the content.

REFLECTION:

Collaborating with the Human Resources Student's Association to create informational packages has taught me to take into consideration the design language of an existing brand, ensuring all elements are coherent with the club's composition.
